What is CrowdCloth?

CrowdCloth is a website and mobile app to receive feedback about what you wear, how you wear it and suggestions on what to wear next.

How does CrowdCloth work?

  1. Tell us about yourself: height, weight, body shape, shirt size, pant length, etc.
  2. Take photos of you in your gear, and tell us about what you're wearing and what you're wearing it to.
  3. Receive feedback and improve your style!

Our Community

That's you! There is a thriving internet community for male fashion advice and we just need to grow and foster it. The goal of the community is to give constructive critisms about what your wearing to help you nail that job interview or have a solid first impression on a first date.

Our Technology

We're taking the knowledge and advice from the community and using machine learning to figure out what are the best clothes for you. Not all clothes are equal, but we should all be able to find clothes that fit our budget, style, and body shape... and we can do it with a proprietary algorithm
